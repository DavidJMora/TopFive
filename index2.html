The Top Five Positions to Play in Quidditch

  1. Snitch
    • Is the Decisive element to how long a game lasts
    • Is allowed to initiate physical contact with players
    • Gets to create their own costume and props
  2. Keeper
    • Gets to put the ball in play after every score
    • Usually the shot caller on the pitch
    • Has special immunity within a certain zone
  3. Chaser
    • Gets to run the Quaffle and score
    • Generally reserved for only the most athletic
    • Gets to tackle people when necessary
  4. Seeker
    • Gets to play multple position in anticipation for release at 18 minutes
    • Is the main element in a win or loss
    • Gets all the credit for a win if they catch the Snitch
  5. Beater
    • Gets to throw dodgeballs(bludger) at people
    • Has to do the least amount of running
    • Depending on skill can be the element in a decisive factor in a win or a loss by defending the Snitch
